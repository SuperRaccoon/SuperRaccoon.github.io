Core Membership

Core members are the heart of ISAB. Along with the officers, they are in charge of planning events based on their committee, and have a bigger involvement than general members. Core members form a more tightly-knit community and social network with the ISAB alumni as well as the officers, and will take an active role in taking ISAB forward through leadership positions. Core members are chosen based on an application and interview process. For more information on applying, please refer to this page.

General Membership

The General Membership program aims to connect the international students at UC Berkeley to each other as well as resources available to them. General membership is geared toward students who would like to have fun with ISAB core members and meet a variety of people in a relaxing way. Membership is $10 for new members and $7 for returning, and will give you access to our monthly social and professional events. Here are some events we have held this semester so far as well as some of our upcoming events!

Our past events include:

- Presidio Food Festival
