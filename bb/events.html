ISAB Spring 2016 Recruitment

Location: First General Meeting | Mofitt 102; First General Social | Wheeler 30

Time: First General Meeting | 7:30 - 9:00PM; First General Social | 7:15 - 9:00 PM

Attention Berkeley Bears! Have you flown on 16 hour flights from the comfort of your gorgeous home to come and study at Berkeley? Do you break out crying watching Korean dramas or hope to road trip around Europe once you graduate? Forget being bilingual, can you speak three languages or four? Or do none of these apply to you, but you simply want to experience and learn about international students and new cultures? If so, come out to International Students Association at Berkeley (ISAB)'s First General Meeting and our First General Social! Refreshments and SLIVER pizza will be served! ISAB is open to everyone, regardless of nationality! Come join us and learn more about our various committees and how you can become a part of the ISAB family!

iLink - An International Alumni Networking Night

Location: Pauley Ballroom, 3rd floor of Martin Luther King Student Union

Time: 7:00PM - 9:00PM

[SIGN UP AT tinyurl.com/iLinkEvent for priority seating!]

International Students' Advisory Committee - ISAC, ISAB - International Students Association at Berkeley, and Office of ASUC Senator Jason Tang are proud to present iLink - An International Alumni Networking Night! International alumni from a variety of industries and companies will be attending to give both international and local students advice on how to find and apply for jobs, what career paths to take, and any other questions you might have. Past Events

SAN FRANCISCO TRIP

At the beginning of the semester, ISAB always hosts a SF trip leading many international students to check out some of the most famous sites in San Francisco such as Pier 39, Fisherman's Wharf, Ghirardelli Square, and the Golden Gate Bridge. It's a fun event and it's a great opportunity to kick off the semester by meeting new international students.

PARTY AT PAPPY'S

Party at Pappy's! We collaborated with Korean American Student Association (KASA) and Taiwanese American Student Association (TASA) to host a fun night at Pappy's with good music and company. It was the perfect event to spend a fun night dancing our worry away with our friends!

PUMPKIN CARVING WITH BIO

There's no better way to celebrate Halloween by carving pumpkins! Every year, ISAB collaborates with Berkeley International Office (BIO) to host the Octoer iMix - Pumpkin Carving at iHouse, which always brings out many international students to socialize and have fun together!

BERKELEY PROJECT

During our Fall 2015 semester, we got our general members and core members together to participate in Berkeley Project! We painted community homes and schools together, having a great time while engaging in making a positive impact around us. It was tough, physical work, but it was definitely rewarding!
