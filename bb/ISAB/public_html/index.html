Welcome to ISAB
Established in 2009, the International Students Association at Berkeley was nominated as a 2010 Outstanding Student Organization and has continued to grow into the largest international student organization at the UC Berkeley campus. Join us, and be a part of the International community at Cal!
Events
We regularly host exciting, large-scale, and multi-cultural events in order to connect with the international community and fellow Berkeley students.
Mentorship
We strive to smooth your transition to Cal through our iBig/iLittle system and mentorship workshops for international and domestic students alike.
Social
We are a close-knit family and regularly have hang outs and dinners, as well as day trips to amusement parks and San Francisco.
