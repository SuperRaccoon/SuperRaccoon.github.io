What We Do
ISAB provides a positive intellectual and social atmosphere for students from diverse backgrounds. We host numerous events to promote awareness of international culture to the campus community, while strengthening Berkeleyâ€™s multicultural community. ISAB provides resources to the international community professionally, socially, and culturally. Current and previous members of ISAB include undergraduate domestic and international students, graduate students, exchange students, graduate student instructors, and the local community. Along with our Officer team, we have 5 committees including External Events, Marketing, Social, Collaborative events, and International Programs.
Events
Want to organize and plan large-scale cultural events for the Berkeley community? Want to meet international students and learn about new cultures? Want to meet and mingle with amazing ISAB members? Come to one of our events! ISAB regularly hosts exciting events, such as World Food Fair, and Meet the Bears, a summer international students orientation, in order to connect with the international community at Berkeley. Our events are usually either free or just a few dollars, and are a great chance to make some new friends from diverse backgrounds.
Mentorship
One of ISAB's core focus is mentorship, both internally and externally. Internally we have the iBig/iLittle system, in which a new member is paired with an older member, allowing for a go-to person for guidance. Externally, our International Programs committee coordinates with the Berkeley International Office to host workshops for international and domestic students alike.
Social
ISAB loves to have fun! Socials are common, both planned and just regular get-togethers and hang outs. In addition to social events such as day trips to amusement parks and dinners, we have our once-per-semester retreat for core members, in which we explore the greater California region and also get to know your fellow ISAB'ers! For general members, we have monthly events that range from exploring food festivals to enjoying bowling nights with a tightly-knit family system!
