Past Events
ISAB RETREAT
We tripped to Santa Cruz for our retreat and had a wonderful time at the Boardwalk Amusement Park. We went on those thrilling rides, ate at a Hawaiian restaurant and we played a lot of games. The retreat was tons of fun and the old and new members got to know each other really well.
SAN FRANCISCO TRIP
At the beginning of the semester, ISAB always hosts a SF trip leading many international students to check out some of the most famous sites in San Francisco such as Pier 39, Fisherman's Wharf, Ghirardelli Square, and the Golden Gate Bridge. It's a fun event and it's a great opportunity to kick off the semester by meeting new international students.
PARTY AT PAPPY'S
Party at Pappy's! Spend a fun night at Pappy's dancing your worry away with your friends.
EAU NIGHT MARKET
Collaborating with EAU and many East Asian student clubs, ISAB participates in this joint event every semester. At the night market, you get to taste some of the most international small cuisines from various cultural clubs. It is one of the most popular events with hundreds of participants every semester.
PUMPKIN CARVING WITH BIO
There's no better way to celebrate Halloween by carving pumpkins! ISAB collaborates with Berkeley International Office (BIO) and presented the iMix - Pumpkin Carving at iHouse.
INTERNATIONAL CUP
Like dodge ball? Like prizes? What's better than spending an afternoon playing dodge ball outdoor? ISAB hosts a dodge ball theme tournament every semester with various prizes. It's another popular event with many cultural groups participating and you definitely would not want to miss out!
